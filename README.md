# Privacy Policy for Gutter Kings
Last Updated: 12/06/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how **BlueCoin Studios** (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the **Gutter Kings** application (“the Game”) on Meta Quest devices and through the Meta Store.

By installing or using the Game, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly
We may collect information you choose to provide, such as:
- Gameplay or bug-report feedback  
- Contact information if you reach out for support (e.g., email)

You are **not required to create an account** with BlueCoin Studios to play Gutter Kings.

---

## 2. Information Automatically Collected

### 2.1 Meta Platform Data
When using a Meta account, Meta may provide us with limited information according to your permissions and Meta’s platform policies. This may include:
- Meta Display Name  
- Meta Account ID  
- Friends list (only if explicitly granted)

We do **not** receive your email address, payment info, or other sensitive Meta account details.

---

### 2.2 In-Game Telemetry (Non-Personal)
To improve performance and stability, Gutter Kings may collect:
- Device type (e.g., Quest 3)  
- Crash logs and performance data  
- Gameplay analytics (e.g., session length, feature usage)  
- Broad region (non-precise)

We do **not** collect precise location or biometric data.

---

### 2.3 Network & Online Gameplay Data
If you use online features (multiplayer, matchmaking, servers), we may process:
- Temporary IP address (for connection only)  
- Session identifiers  
- Player interactions with game servers  

We do **not** use this data to personally identify players.

---

## 3. How We Use the Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and operate Gutter Kings  
- Maintain server and network functionality  
- Fix bugs, crashes, and performance issues  
- Improve player experience and gameplay balance  
- Respond to support requests  

We do **not** sell, rent, or monetize data.

---

## 4. Sharing of Information

We may share minimal information with:
- **Service providers** (e.g., server hosting, analytics)  
- **Meta Platforms**, as required for platform features  
- **Law enforcement**, only if required by law  

We do **not** share user data with advertisers or data brokers.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain logs and telemetry only as long as necessary for:
- Game functionality  
- Performance and security  
- Legal compliance  

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

Gutter Kings does **not** knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region).  
If such information is discovered, we will delete it promptly.

Meta may restrict access based on age according to their policies.

---

## 7. Third-Party Services

Gutter Kings may use third-party services, including:
- Meta Platform SDK  
- Crash reporting or analytics tools  
- Online server hosting  

These services receive only the information required to operate their function and are not permitted to misuse or sell your data.
